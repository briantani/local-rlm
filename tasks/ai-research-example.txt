# AI Research Task: State of the Art Analysis (2020-2025)

## Objective
Conduct a comprehensive analysis of artificial intelligence advancements between 2020 and 2025, producing a detailed report with visualizations, data tables, and trend analysis.

## Deliverables

### 1. Executive Summary
- High-level overview of key breakthroughs
- Major paradigm shifts identified
- Impact assessment across industries

### 2. Technical Deep Dive

#### Language Models
- Evolution from GPT-3 (2020) to current state-of-the-art
- Model size trends and efficiency improvements
- Breakthrough architectures (Transformers, Mamba, etc.)
- Context length improvements (4K â†’ 1M+ tokens)

#### Computer Vision
- Vision Transformers (ViT) adoption
- Diffusion models (DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Midjourney)
- Multi-modal capabilities (CLIP, GPT-4V, Gemini)

#### Reinforcement Learning
- AlphaFold 2 and protein folding
- Game-playing agents (Dota 2, StarCraft II)
- Robotics applications

### 3. Data Analysis Requirements

Generate the following visualizations:
- Timeline chart of major model releases
- Bar chart comparing model parameters over time
- Line graph showing compute costs trends
- Heat map of AI adoption by industry sector

### 4. Trend Identification

Analyze and identify:
- Emerging research directions
- Convergence of modalities (text, image, audio, video)
- Open-source vs. proprietary models dynamics
- Safety and alignment research trends

### 5. Future Projections

Provide speculative analysis on:
- Expected capabilities by 2027-2030
- Potential bottlenecks (compute, data, architecture)
- Societal impact predictions
- Regulatory landscape evolution

## Methodology

1. Research publicly available papers, benchmarks, and announcements
2. Synthesize findings into coherent narrative
3. Generate visualizations using matplotlib/seaborn
4. Create markdown tables for quantitative comparisons
5. Provide citations and references

## Output Format

Produce a comprehensive markdown report (~5000-7000 words) with:
- Clear section headings
- Embedded data visualizations (saved as PNG)
- Comparison tables
- Citation section with links to sources
- Executive summary at the beginning
- Conclusions and future outlook at the end

## Success Criteria

- Comprehensive coverage of the 2020-2025 period
- At least 5 high-quality visualizations
- Minimum 3 comparison tables with quantitative data
- Insightful analysis beyond mere fact reporting
- Clear writing suitable for technical audience
- Professional formatting throughout
